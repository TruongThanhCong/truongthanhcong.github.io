Thanh Cong Truong is currently working at the Faculty of Informatics Technology of University of Finance - Marketing, Ho Chi Minh city, Viet Nam (UFM). He obtained his MSc. in Computer Science at the University of Information Technology, Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City, and Ph.D. at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, VŠB - Technical University of Ostrava (VŠB-TU). His primary research relates to Artificial intelligence, swarm intelligence algorithms, and cybersecurity. He has published numerous research articles in peer-reviewed journals and international conferences.
